Your Trusted Partner in Temperature and Humidity Control Solutions

Rehoboth Enviro Systems Pvt. Ltd. specializes in manufacturing high-quality desiccant dehumidification air handlers for both commercial and industrial applications. Our mission is to deliver top-notch, energy-efficient desiccant dehumidifiers that precisely meet our customers' needs, ensuring optimal performance and satisfaction.

Founded in 2011 by a dynamic team of young entrepreneurs, REHOBOTH began its journey in Mumbai with a vision to lead the HVAC solutions industry. From humble beginnings, REHOBOTH has grown into a globally recognized brand, renowned for our innovation and quality in desiccant dehumidification.

With our headquarters in Mumbai and a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, we offer comprehensive solutions for temperature and humidity control, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications with our state-of-the-art desiccant dehumidifiers and exceptional service.

Industrial Dehumidifier Manufacturing

As pioneers in advanced desiccant dehumidification technology, we design and manufacture cutting-edge solutions for effective moisture control, ensuring superior quality and performance.

Spares and Services

Our comprehensive portfolio includes high-quality spare parts for all makes of desiccant dehumidifiers, guaranteeing seamless operation and longevity of your systems. Additionally, we offer routine maintenance and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure optimal performance.
