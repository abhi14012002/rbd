Our Story

Leading Innovation in Humidity Control

Established with a vision to provide cutting-edge humidity control solutions, Rehoboth Enviro Systems has been at the forefront of desiccant dehumidification technology for over 25 years. We specialize in designing, manufacturing, and supplying advanced dehumidification systems that serve diverse industries across the globe.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable humidity control solutions. From compact units for small applications to large-scale engineered systems, we deliver excellence in every project.
